Questions

Do I need coding experience?
No. We start from zero. You'll learn to use AI to write code, but you don't need to be a programmer. If you can follow instructions and are willing to learn, you can do this.
How much time do I need each week?
Plan for 5-8 hours per week: one live session plus time to complete that week's work. This is designed for people with jobs — not a full-time commitment.
What if I can't make a live session?
All sessions are recorded. You'll have lifetime access to the recordings and can catch up async. The Skool community is there for questions between calls.
Will I actually land a client in 4 weeks?
Possibly — but I can't promise that. Sales takes time. What I can promise: by the end, you'll have the skills, a portfolio, and active outreach started. You'll be pitching real prospects.
Why is this better than a bootcamp or YouTube?
Bootcamps cost $10-15k and take months. YouTube is free but scattered — you piece things together alone. PathForge is focused, fast, and includes the sales side most courses ignore.
What tools will I need?
A computer and internet connection. All the tools we use are free: VS Code, GitHub, Formspree, Google Analytics. No expensive software subscriptions.
